Garden Wall at Lavaderos

Oil on Linen
8"X6" | December 8, 2025

This painting was started en plein air at the Lavaderos del Chorro 'Artist's corner' in San Miguel de Allende, Mexico. The Lavaderos del Chorro is a historical public laundry site and communal space, originally constructed to utilize the natural spring water on location.
