Skull & Toothbrush

Oil on panel
27"X13" | June 14, 2024

A medium-sized still life in oils featuring a skull with a toothbrush in a jar. I built the frame from reclaimed fir.

This piece was part of the "Post Op" group show at Brassworks Gallery in Portland, Oregon, which exhibited from June 14th, 2025 to July 2025.
